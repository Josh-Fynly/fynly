# FOUNDING

## Project Genesis

This document marks the beginning of FYNLY.

FYNLY was founded on a simple belief:

Technology should solve meaningful problems and continue creating value long after the excitement of its release has faded.

This repository represents more than a website.

It represents the beginning of an engineering philosophy.

---

## Why FYNLY Exists

To build intelligent systems that improve lives.

To promote disciplined engineering.

To document knowledge.

To create standards.

To leave every system better than it was found.

---

## What We Believe

Engineering is responsibility.

Architecture matters.

Security begins with design.

Quality is intentional.

Integrity is non-negotiable.

Learning never ends.

---

## Our Commitment

Every decision made under the FYNLY name should answer one question:

"Will this still make sense years from now?"

If the answer is no,

we redesign.
