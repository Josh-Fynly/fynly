# ADR-003

## Title

Homepage Before Pages

---

## Status

Accepted

---

## Context

Many websites are built page by page without considering the overall visitor experience.

This often results in disconnected sections and weak storytelling.

---

## Decision

The homepage shall be designed as a guided narrative.

Every section must prepare the visitor for the next one.

Content order is intentional.

---

## Consequences

Positive

- Better engagement
- Stronger storytelling
- Higher clarity
- Easier expansion

Trade-offs

- Requires planning
- Sections cannot be rearranged arbitrarily

---

## Conclusion

The homepage becomes the front door to the FYNLY ecosystem rather than a collection of unrelated content.
