# ADR-004

## Title

Consistency Before Creativity

---

## Status

Accepted

---

## Context

Visual inconsistency weakens trust.

As the FYNLY ecosystem grows, different products, documents, and contributors will participate in the brand.

Without a documented visual identity, each project risks developing its own appearance.

---

## Decision

FYNLY adopts a unified Visual Identity Standard.

Every interface, document, presentation, social graphic, and future product must conform to this standard.

---

## Consequences

Positive

- Strong brand recognition
- Faster design decisions
- Consistent user experience
- Easier onboarding for contributors

Trade-offs

- Less freedom for arbitrary experimentation
- Requires periodic review

---

## Conclusion

Consistency strengthens trust.

Trust strengthens institutions.
